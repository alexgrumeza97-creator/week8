3. Framework Recommendation
Recommended Approach: Scrum + XP
Why Scrum?

Scrum provides project management support through:

Product Backlog
Sprint Planning
Sprint Reviews
Sprint Retrospectives
Incremental delivery
Why XP?

XP strengthens technical quality through:

Test Driven Development (TDD)
Refactoring
Pair Programming
Continuous Integration
Justification

Combining Scrum and XP supports rapid MVP delivery while ensuring maintainable, reliable software. Scrum manages project workflow and stakeholder engagement, while XP improves development quality and reduces defects.
